# Intent: Build A Season Habit

## Pillar

Community And Growth

## User Story

I want the group to come back next week.

## Success Signal

The app shows season progress, upcoming nights, and reasons to return.

## Product Principles

- Weekly repetition should be visible.
- Players should have reasons to check the app between nights.
- Seasons should create friendly competition without making setup harder.
- Past nights should feed the next night.

## Feature Backlog

- Season leaderboard.
- Past nights timeline.
- Monthly champion.
- Most improved award.
- Attendance streaks.
- Next night planning.

## Data Notes

- Season data can initially derive from group sessions and player stats.
- Later seasons may need explicit season records.
- Keep per-group seasons separate.

## Verification

- Completed nights appear in group context.
- Rankings update after each night.
- New season reset does not delete historical data.
- Local fallback remains usable.
